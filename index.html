Our Story

Café Era began with a simple idea: to create a cozy sanctuary where people could find refuge from the chaos of daily life. Founded by Joy Dutt Sharma, our cafe quickly became a beloved community hub, where friends gather, strangers become acquaintances,and cherished memories are made over cups of exceptional coffee

  • Known for exceptional coffee and warm atmosphere.
  • Where friends gather, strangers become acquaintances, and memories are made.
  • A testament to the power of community and meaningful moments shared over coffee.

Our Vision

At Café Era, our vision is to cultivate a vibrant community hub where every gues feels embraced, inspired, and uplifted. We aspire to be more than just a coffee destination; we aim to be a catalyst for connection, creativity, and meaningful conversations.

  • Foster a vibrant community hub that promotes connection and inspiration.
  • Provide exceptional service, quality ingredients, and sustainable practices.
  • Celebrate diversity and create an inclusive environment for all.
  • Serve as a catalyst for meaningful conversations and relationships.
